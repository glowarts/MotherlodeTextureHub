Style guide & rules

Before you start

  • 16×16 pixels, PNG, transparency allowed. Anything else gets rejected automatically.
  • Match the neighbours. Every texture page shows the rest of its material family and the same variant across other materials. Your texture has to sit next to those without looking out of place.
  • Keep the palette tight. Most existing textures use 6–12 colours. Avoid gradients and anti-aliasing; this is pixel art, not a downscaled render.
  • No AI-generated textures, no ripping from other mods or resource packs. Submissions must be your own work.
  • By submitting you agree your texture can ship in Motherlode under the mod's licence, credited to you.

Priority

Missing means the block exists in game with no texture at all — most valuable. Placeholder means it currently uses a flat colour or a copy of another texture — also very valuable. Unreviewed has real art that hasn't been signed off. Done is finished — only redraw it if you think you can clearly beat it.
